= Chad_Hugo = 
Charles Edward Hugo (born February 24, 1974) is a Filipino-American record producer, multi-instrumentalist, singer and songwriter. With close friend Pharrell Williams, he formed the production duo the Neptunes in the early 1990s, with whom he has produced songs for many recording artists. He is also a member of the band N.E.R.D, alongside Williams and Shay Haley.


== Career ==
Hugo and Williams were announced to be inducted into the Songwriters Hall of Fame as a part of the 2020 class.
